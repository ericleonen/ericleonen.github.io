These quotes stay here so long as I can remember where they came from:
1. > Enjoy the side trips. A lot, okay?
   > Things that are more important than what you're hunting for could be right there by the side of the road.
2. > It is only with the heart that one can see rightly.
   > What is essential is invisible to the eye.
3. > When the facts change, I change my mind—what do you do, sir?
4. > Stuff your eyes with wonder, live as if you'd drop dead in ten seconds.
   > See the world.
   > It's more fantastic than any dream made or paid for in factories.
   > Ask no guarantees, ask for no security, there never was such an animal.
   > And if there were, it would be related to the great sloth which hangs upside down in a tree all day every day, sleeping its life away.
   > To hell with that shake the tree and knock the great sloth down on his ass.
5. > But I don't want comfort.
   > I want God, I want poetry, I want real danger, I want freedom, I want goodness.
   > I want sin.
6. > I would rather discover one true cause than gain the kingdom of Persia.
7. > Far to the west, across the sea, there is a place called Vinland.
   > It is warm and fertile, far from slavery and the flames of war.
   > No one can reach you there.
