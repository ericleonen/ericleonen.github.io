Built a semantic search engine for mathematical theorems as part of the University of Washington's [Math AI Lab](https://sites.math.washington.edu/ai/). Led development of the world's largest theorem database (9.2 million) using AWS services and a Python pipeline. Accepted to the 2026 ICLR Workshop and submitted to 2026 ICML.
